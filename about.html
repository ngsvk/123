ABOUT US

Welcome to Sri Sathya Sai School & PU College Mysuru

Our institution is dedicated to fostering Education in Human Values. Today's education system often prioritizes intellectual and skill development while neglecting the cultivation of good character. We believe that true education extends beyond academics to encompass moral and spiritual growth. Our mission is to nurture students who not only excel academically but also possess compassion, humility, and a sense of service towards society.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that develops the mind, body, and spirit of every student. We aim to cultivate young minds with strong moral values, academic excellence, and a deep sense of social responsibility, preparing them to become compassionate leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that seamlessly integrates academic excellence with human values education. We envision creating a generation of students who are intellectually competent, emotionally balanced, spiritually grounded, and socially committed.

Our Heritage

67 years of excellence in education and character building

Established in 1957

Our institution was founded in 1957 by the late Smt. Sunandamma, who was deeply inspired by the teachings and philosophy of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba. What began as a small school with a handful of students has grown into a respected educational institution serving hundreds of families in Mysuru and surrounding areas.

Over the decades, we have remained committed to our founding principles of integrating academic excellence with moral and spiritual education. Our alumni have gone on to become successful professionals, entrepreneurs, and leaders in various fields, carrying forward the values they learned within these walls.
